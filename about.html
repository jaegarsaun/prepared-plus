About us

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the shelf life on the meals?

A: To keep food out of the Danger Zone, we recomend to keep food cold, at or below 4degC or frozen below 0degC to maintain freshness.

Q: What if I miss the pickup window to get my meals?

A: It is your respomsibility to pick up your meals. If you miss the pickup window we do not provide refunds sine all the meals are made to order.

Q: How long should I microwabe each meal?

A: If the meal has been in the refrigerator, microwave for 1-3 minutes. (Times will vary.) If you freeze the meal, move it to the refrigerator 12-24 hours before re-heating. All meals should reach an internal temperature of 165degC before consuming.

Q: What is your cancellation/refund policy?

A: We understand that plans change. If you request to cancel your orders before out deadline, you will receive a full refund or credit for the following menu. We do not offer refunds after the ordering deadline since our meals are made to order. If you are not satisfied with a meal or it has been damaged you may return the meal and we will issue a full refund.

Q: What do the calories in each meal range from?

A: All of our meals range from approximately 300-600 calories. We can customize calories and macronutrients for each meal at a fee. All meals are weighed out. However serving size may vary.
